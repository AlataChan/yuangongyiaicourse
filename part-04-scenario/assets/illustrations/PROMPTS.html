图 4：五角色协作流水线

  • 输出路径：course/part-04-scenario/assets/illustrations/qinglan-agent-pipeline.png
  • 用途：解释「一个节点只做一种判断」，并将 AI 产出与人工发布权分开。
Use case: stylized-concept. Asset type: wide horizontal 1.9:1 labeled material illustration for a Chinese course webpage, designed for a 936x500 image well. Primary request: A clear left-to-right multi-agent editorial pipeline built from six small physical stations on one continuous tabletop rail. Station 1 is a routing switch receiving an urgent task card; station 2 compares source documents with a magnifying glass; station 3 arranges outline blocks; station 4 types a draft page; station 5 inspects the draft with a checklist and shield; station 6 is a visibly human hand opening a final publication gate. Each AI station passes a compact blue task packet to the next station. The final gate must visibly remain human-controlled. Chinese labels: Add exactly six short Simplified Chinese labels as clean printed callouts: "主控", "核事实", "定结构", "写初稿", "审风险", "人确认". Place each label directly below or above its matching station in a single horizontal line; large, high-contrast, readable, and away from edges. Style/medium: clean Swiss editorial 3D material illustration, off-white background, black ink outlines, refined white and light-gray physical models, one vivid IKB blue accent (#002FA7) for the rail, task packets, arrows and active blocks. Composition/framing: wide 1.9:1 pipeline, all six stations fully visible, centered vertically, generous safe margins, no crop. Lighting/mood: crisp studio light, calm credible workplace teaching tone. Constraints: no extra words, no English, no numbers, no logos, no watermark, no page title, no fake app UI, no cute robots, no decorative blobs, no gradient background.

图 5：审核退回与人审放行闭环

  • 输出路径：course/part-04-scenario/assets/illustrations/qinglan-review-loop.png
  • 用途：解释多智能体协作不是只向前传递，而是能根据问题类型退回负责节点，最终仍停在人工闸门。
Use case: stylized-concept. Asset type: wide horizontal 1.9:1 labeled material illustration for a Chinese course webpage, designed for a 936x500 image well. Primary request: A goal-and-evaluator collaboration loop shown with physical editorial objects. A draft page enters a neutral review gate at center. One return rail loops left to a source-check station when facts are missing. A second short return rail loops to a writing station when content risk or tone problems are found. A small red stop block prevents the draft from moving forward while either issue remains. Only a blue passed packet can move right to a separate gate operated by a human hand; it must not move directly to a public broadcast symbol. Chinese labels: Add exactly four short Simplified Chinese labels as clean printed callouts: "缺事实", "有风险", "局部返工", "人审放行". Place labels next to the correct return rail, stop block and human gate; keep all labels horizontal, large, high-contrast, readable, and away from edges. Style/medium: clean Swiss editorial 3D material illustration, off-white background, black ink outlines, refined white and light-gray physical surfaces, one vivid IKB blue accent (#002FA7) for the approved path and return rails, with only a small Signal Red (#E60012) stop block for the blocker. Composition/framing: wide 1.9:1 loop, full loop and human gate visible, centered vertically, generous safe margins, no crop. Lighting/mood: crisp studio light, calm analytical risk-control tone. Constraints: no extra words, no English, no numbers, no logos, no watermark, no page title, no fake app UI, no cute robots, no decorative blobs, no gradient background.

元公益工作室
